About

This wesite aims to track the use of traffic calming features and traffic collisions in Toronto. We believe it is important to keep track of past locations of accidents to make sure we can prevent future injuries and fatalities. We are working to keep the City of Toronto accountable in its Vision Zero Promises. To read about the city's Vision Zero commitments, click here.

How to interpret the map:


The yellow dots represent major collisions (serious imjuries or fatalities)
The red dots represent red light cameras.
The faint lines show the pedestrian network.

All our data is from the City of Toronto's Open Data portal. To find more about this map, we have linked these data sets below:
